Project Overview

The Safe Disposal of Car Tires Project is an innovative environmental solution designed to address the growing challenge of waste car tires through a clean and controlled thermal processing technology.

The project focuses on converting used car tires into valuable industrial products without toxic emissions, smoke, or airborne pollutants, while supporting circular economy principles and national sustainability goals.

Background & Environmental Challenge

The Global Problem

The accumulation of waste car tires is a significant and ongoing environmental issue affecting more than 25 cities worldwide.

Project Solution

The project utilizes a controlled thermal processing technology (Pyrolysis) operating in an oxygen-free environment to safely process waste car tires and recover valuable reusable materials.
